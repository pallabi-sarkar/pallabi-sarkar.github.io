5. Key Insights

  • Organic traffic generates the highest quality users based on conversion rate.
  • Paid social traffic drives volume but lacks conversion efficiency.
  • Direct traffic shows strong user intent and engagement.
  • There is an opportunity to optimize paid landing pages.

6. Business Recommendations

  • Increase focus on SEO to strengthen organic acquisition.
  • Optimize paid campaigns by refining audience targeting.
  • Improve landing page experience for social traffic users.
  • Allocate budget toward high-performing channels.

7. Conclusion

The analysis indicates that Organic and Direct channels drive high-quality traffic, while Paid Social requires optimization to improve ROI. Strategic budget allocation and landing page improvements can enhance overall performance.
