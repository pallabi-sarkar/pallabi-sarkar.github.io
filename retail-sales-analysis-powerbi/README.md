# Retail Sales Analysis Using Power BI

## Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data to identify customer behavior, product performance, and sales trends. The goal is to generate actionable business insights that can help management improve sales performance and make data-driven decisions.

The analysis was performed using Microsoft Excel and Power BI.

---

## Business Problem

A retail store wants to better understand:

* Which product categories generate the most revenue
* Which customer segments contribute the most sales
* How sales performance changes over time
* Which business opportunities can drive future growth

The objective is to transform raw transaction data into meaningful business insights and recommendations.

---

## Dataset Information

The dataset contains retail transaction records with the following fields:

* Transaction ID
* Customer ID
* Gender
* Age
* Product Category
* Quantity
* Price per Unit
* Total Amount
* Date

Dataset Size:

* Records: 1,000
* Columns: 9

## Data Analysis Process

### 1. Data Understanding

The dataset was explored to understand customer demographics, product categories, transaction details, and revenue generation patterns.

### 2. KPI Development

The following KPIs were created:

* Total Revenue
* Total Transactions
* Total Quantity Sold
* Average Order Value

### 3. Product Analysis

The analysis focused on:

* Revenue by Product Category
* Quantity Sold by Product Category
* Category Performance Comparison

### 4. Customer Analysis

Customer behavior was analyzed using:

* Revenue by Age Group
* Revenue by Gender
* Revenue by Age Group and Product Category

### 5. Sales Trend Analysis

Monthly sales trends were examined to identify high-performing and low-performing periods.

## Key Findings

### Product Performance

* Electronics generated the highest revenue (₹156,905).
* Clothing generated nearly the same revenue (₹155,580).
* Clothing recorded the highest sales volume with 894 units sold.
* Beauty was the lowest-performing category in both revenue and quantity sold.

### Customer Insights

* Mature customers (46+) generated ₹187,425 in revenue.
* Mature customers contributed approximately 41% of total revenue.
* Mature customers showed the strongest preference for Electronics.
* Adults (26–35) showed the strongest preference for Clothing.

### Sales Trends

* May recorded the highest monthly revenue (₹53,150).
* September recorded the lowest monthly revenue (₹23,620).
* Revenue showed noticeable fluctuations throughout the year.

---

## Business Recommendations

### Recommendation 1

Focus customer retention efforts on the Mature customer segment because they contribute the highest share of revenue.

### Recommendation 2

Continue investing in Electronics while monitoring Clothing performance, as both categories generate similar revenue.

### Recommendation 3

Investigate the causes of low sales in September to identify potential operational or demand-related issues.

### Recommendation 4

Develop targeted promotional campaigns for Beauty products to improve category performance.

### Recommendation 5

Use customer segmentation strategies to create age-group-specific marketing campaigns.
