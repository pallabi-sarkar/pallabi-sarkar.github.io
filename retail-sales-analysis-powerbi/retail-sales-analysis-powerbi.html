Retail Sales Analysis Using Power BI

Business Intelligence Project focused on customer behavior, product performance, and sales trend analysis.

Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data to identify customer purchasing patterns, top-performing product categories, and revenue trends.

The objective was to transform raw transaction data into actionable business insights and recommendations using Excel and Power BI.

Business Questions

  • Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
  • Which customer segments contribute the most sales?
  • How does sales performance change over time?
  • What actions can management take to improve performance?

Tools Used

Excel Power BI DAX Pivot Tables Data Visualization Business Analysis

Dashboard Screenshots

Key Findings

Product Performance

  • Electronics generated the highest revenue.
  • Clothing generated nearly equal revenue.
  • Clothing recorded the highest sales volume.
  • Beauty was the lowest-performing category.

Customer Insights

  • Mature customers generated 41% of total revenue.
  • Electronics was the preferred category among mature customers.
  • Adults showed the strongest preference for clothing.

Sales Trends

  • May recorded the highest revenue.
  • September recorded the lowest revenue.
  • Revenue fluctuated significantly throughout the year.

Business Recommendations

  1. Focus customer retention efforts on mature customers.
  2. Continue investing in Electronics while monitoring Clothing performance.
  3. Investigate September's sales decline.
  4. Increase promotional activity for Beauty products.
  5. Implement customer-segment-specific marketing campaigns.
