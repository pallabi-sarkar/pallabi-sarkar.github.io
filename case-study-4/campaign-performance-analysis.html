Campaign Performance Analysis (Device-Level)

1. Business Objective

The objective of this analysis is to evaluate campaign performance across devices and identify high-performing combinations based on revenue, conversion rate, and ROAS. The goal is to optimize budget allocation and improve overall marketing efficiency.

2. Data Overview

The dataset includes campaign-level and device-level metrics such as clicks, cost, conversions, and revenue. Key performance indicators calculated:

  • Conversion Rate
  • Cost per Acquisition (CPA)
  • Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)

3. Revenue by Campaign & Device

Desktop users generate higher revenue for Email and Google Search campaigns, while social campaigns are more mobile-driven.

4. Conversion Rate by Device

Desktop traffic shows stronger conversion efficiency across most campaigns, indicating better purchase completion behavior.

5. ROAS Comparison

Email Campaign demonstrates the highest ROAS, particularly on desktop, suggesting high-quality targeted traffic.

6. Key Insights

  • Email Campaign (Desktop) is the most profitable channel-device combination.
  • Mobile traffic generates higher volume but lower conversion efficiency.
  • Social campaigns require mobile UX optimization to improve ROI.
  • Google Search performs consistently across both devices.

7. Business Recommendations

  • Increase budget allocation to high-ROAS desktop campaigns.
  • Optimize mobile landing pages and checkout flow.
  • Improve audience targeting for social campaigns.
  • Use device-level retargeting strategies.
