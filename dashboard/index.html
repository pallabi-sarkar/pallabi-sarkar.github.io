Case Study

1. Objective

The objective of this analysis was to evaluate overall website performance, analyze traffic sources, understand monthly trends, and identify device-level behavior to uncover optimization opportunities.

2. Tools Used

  • Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, KPI Calculations)
  • Data Visualization (Bar, Line, Pie Charts)
  • Business Performance Metrics

3. Key Insights

  • Mobile drives higher traffic volume but lower conversion efficiency.
  • Paid campaigns show strong conversion contribution.
  • Overall sessions show stable growth trend.

4. Business Recommendations

  • Improve mobile user journey to increase conversion rate.
  • Scale high-performing paid campaigns.
  • Optimize landing pages to boost engagement.
