Case Study 3: Conversion Funnel Analysis

1. Objective

To analyze user drop-offs in the conversion funnel and identify optimization opportunities to improve overall purchase rate.

2. Funnel Overview

Landing Page

10,000 Users

↓

Product Page

6,000 Users

Drop-off: 40%

↓

Checkout

2,500 Users

Drop-off: 58%

↓

Purchase

1,800 Users

Drop-off: 28%

3. Drop-Off Analysis

  • Landing → Product: 40% drop
  • Product → Checkout: 58% drop (Highest)
  • Checkout → Purchase: 28% drop

The biggest drop occurs between Product Page and Checkout, indicating potential friction in pricing, trust, or CTA placement.

4. Business Recommendations

  • Improve product page CTA visibility.
  • Add trust badges and customer reviews.
  • Simplify checkout process (reduce form fields).
  • Enable guest checkout option.
  • Test pricing visibility and discount placement.

5. Key Insight

Overall conversion rate = 18% (1,800 / 10,000). Optimization at Product Page stage can significantly increase revenue.
