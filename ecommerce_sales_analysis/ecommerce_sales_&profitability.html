E-Commerce Sales & Profitability Analysis

SQL Server + Power BI Business Analytics Project

Project Overview

This project analyzes e-commerce sales transactions to evaluate business performance, product profitability, customer segments, and regional sales trends. Using SQL Server for data cleaning and business analysis and Power BI for interactive dashboard development, the project provides valuable insights to support strategic business decisions.

Data Cleaning & Transformation

  • Removed duplicate records.
  • Checked for missing values.
  • Verified data types.
  • Created Month column for trend analysis.
  • Created Profit Margin KPI.
  • Standardized category names.
  • Validated revenue and profit calculations.
  • Built relationships for reporting.

Business Problem

The company wants to understand:

  • How healthy is overall business performance?
  • Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
  • Which products generate the highest profit?
  • How do discounts impact profitability?
  • Which customer segment contributes the most revenue?
  • Which cities have the strongest sales performance?
  • How can overall profitability be improved?

Key Findings

Product Performance

  • Dresses generate the highest revenue.
  • Dresses also generate the highest profit.
  • T-Shirts achieve one of the highest profit margins.
  • Top-selling products contribute a large percentage of revenue.
  • Product performance is relatively balanced across categories.

    • Profitability Analysis

  • Mumbai represents the strongest regional market.
  • Accessories provide a strong balance between revenue and margin.
  • Monthly profit remains relatively stable.
  • Revenue growth closely follows profit growth.

    • Customer Segments Analysis

  • B2B customers contribute nearly two-thirds of total revenue.
  • B2B customers generate almost twice the profit compared to B2C.
  • The company should prioritize retaining B2B customers.
  • B2C still presents an opportunity for future expansion through targeted promotions.

    • Executive Recommendations

    • Increase investment in Dresses because they generate the highest revenue and profit.
    • Expand B2B customer acquisition through loyalty programs and corporate partnerships.
    • Improve sales of lower-performing product categories using targeted promotions.
    • Focus marketing campaigns in Mumbai and Bangalore where profitability is strongest.
    • Avoid excessive discounting since higher discounts do not consistently improve profits.
    • Increase inventory for top-selling products to prevent stock shortages.
    • Monitor monthly sales trends to identify seasonal demand patterns.
    • Continue tracking profit margins alongside revenue rather than focusing solely on sales growth.

    Project Impact

    This project demonstrates how SQL and Power BI can be used to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor revenue, profitability, customer behavior, and regional performance, supporting informed business decisions and continuous growth.
