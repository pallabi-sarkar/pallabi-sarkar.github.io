Case Study: Website Performance Dashboard

1. Objective

The objective of this analysis was to evaluate overall website performance, analyze traffic sources, understand monthly trends, and identify device-level behavior to uncover optimization opportunities.

2. Tools Used

  • Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, KPI Calculations)
  • Data Visualization (Bar, Line, Pie Charts)
  • Basic Business Performance Metrics

3. Key Performance Indicators

  • Total Sessions: 12,540
  • Total Users: 9,870
  • Conversion Rate: 4.8%
  • Engagement Rate: 62%

4. Traffic Source Analysis

Organic and Paid channels contribute significantly to overall sessions. However, Paid traffic shows slightly stronger conversion contribution, indicating effective campaign targeting.

5. Monthly Sessions Trend

Monthly analysis shows stable growth across periods, suggesting consistent acquisition performance and steady user engagement.

6. Device Breakdown

Mobile generates higher traffic volume compared to Desktop, but desktop users show relatively stronger conversion efficiency, highlighting a potential mobile UX optimization opportunity.

7. Key Business Insights & Recommendations

  • Improve mobile conversion flow to increase overall conversion rate.
  • Scale paid campaigns with high-performing targeting segments.
  • Optimize landing pages for higher engagement and reduced drop-off.
